## Field Notes

- The **Competitive Alternatives** table must have at least 3 entries. "Do nothing" counts as one.
- **Unique Attributes** must reference specific alternatives by name — "better than everyone" is not acceptable.
- **Value** must show the chain: attribute → capability → outcome. Features alone are not value.
- **Customer Segments** must describe who actually cares, not the total addressable market.
- **Market Frame** must explain why it was chosen over alternatives.
- **Skip Flags** are added automatically when the user skips a step after the warn-and-flag enforcement level.
